    Hello, I'm Felix Mukungu

    A Co-Founder of Bitcoin Nairobi and Cryptocurrency Educator, a prominent figure in the Nairobi cryptocurrency community, known for his dedication to education and youth empowerment through Bitcoin and blockchain technology. As a co-founder of Bitcoin Nairobi, Felix plays a crucial role in guiding the organization's vision and activities.

    Felix Mukungu's work in the cryptocurrency space is characterized by his commitment to education, community building, and financial inclusion. Through his efforts, he is helping to shape a future where Bitcoin and blockchain technology are accessible and beneficial to all., Co-Founder @BitcoinNairobi: Felix co-leads an initiative focused on Bitcoin education and adoption in Nairobi. Educator and Mentor: Dedicates his time to teaching and mentoring youth in cryptocurrency. Community Builder: Works to connect and support the local cryptocurrency community. Advocate for Financial Inclusion: Promotes Bitcoin as a tool for financial inclusion. Active on Social Media: Engages a broad audience with educational content and insights on Bitcoin.
